## No Bullet Bill Cancel When Touching Bottom of Rainbow Road

When a Bullet touches the bottom of the road in Rainbow Road, it cancels out and ends. This code removes this.

<details>
<summary>NTSC-U</summary>

```powerpc
045B0E18 60000000
```
</details>

<details>
<summary>PAL</summary>

```powerpc
0459BE30 60000000
```
</details>
